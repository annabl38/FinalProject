Cuisine Finder

Our persistent group of hungry data analysists with worldly tastes in popular cuisines wanted to see if we could create a machine learning model that could pit itself against our persnickety pallets. After reviewing kaggle.com for machine learning competitions we found a data set that fit our neurotic needs.

India’s market is perhaps the most colorful, awash in the rich hues and aromas of dozens of spices: turmeric, star anise, poppy seeds, and garam masala as far as the eye can see.Some of our strongest geographic and cultural associations are tied to aregion's local foods.
